Key Observations for Procurement
TI: Cost leader via 300mm
40% unit cost advantage vs 200mm confirmed by management. Basis for requesting cost pass-through in negotiation — grounded in TI's own 10-K disclosure.
STM: Most transparent
Best process disclosure of all five suppliers — BCD and VIPpower confirmed per fab. Qualification and supply chain risk assessment is easier for STM than for TI.
IFX: Broadest wide bandgap
Only supplier to confirm Si, SiC and GaN gate driver capability in one filing. SiC/GaN gate driver technology lead creates a real switching cost — factor into negotiation.
NXP: Indirect TSMC exposure
SSMC Singapore (61.2% NXP / TSMC JV) is the likely manufacturing site for NXP power products. Same Taiwan Strait exposure as ADI — less visible but equally real.
ADI: Highest foundry risk
ADI is the only supplier to explicitly flag Taiwan Strait risk in its annual filing. Fab-lite model maximises margins but creates maximum foundry dependency if TSMC is disrupted.
Product nodes need PPAP data
Specific process nodes (e.g. 130nm BCD) are not disclosed in any public filing. Part-level process and approved site data requires PPAP package analysis — a Phase 2 capability.
